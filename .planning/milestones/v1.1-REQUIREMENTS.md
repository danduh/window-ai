## Adjustments / Notes

- **Phase 5 Pick button clipping** — Discovered during Phase 5 UAT; resolved in Phase 7 via ResizeObserver target switch (`document.body` instead of `document.documentElement`) + `Math.max(scrollHeight, offsetHeight)` + `min-height: 200px` safety net in carousel template.
- **bridge.ts dev-gate deviation** — Plan 07-02 executor initially used `import.meta.env.DEV` (Vite-only). Webpack-dev-server failed to compile (13 TS2339 errors). Fixed in commit `00fb8ea` by replacing with `IS_DEV = typeof process !== 'undefined' && process.env.NODE_ENV !== 'production'` constant (mirroring `chat/src/app/config/analytics.ts` convention).
- **Chat panel cloned, not refactored** — `GenUIChatPanel.tsx` is a full clone of `AgentDrawer.tsx`'s JSON-dispatch loop per brownfield discipline. The two files share patterns but no code. Acceptable duplication for a coarse-granularity milestone.
