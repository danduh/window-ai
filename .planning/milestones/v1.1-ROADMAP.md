## Architectural Decisions Preserved

- Native `navigator.modelContext` only (no `@mcp-b/global` polyfill) — carries from v1.0
- Chrome 147 `LanguageModel.create({tools})` codepath still broken — Phase 6 reuses `responseFormat` JSON-dispatch from `AgentDrawer.tsx`
- Brownfield discipline maintained: no edits to `/webmcp`, `RecipeWorkbench/*`, `mcp/`, `mcp-client/`, `devops/awsweb/`
- Module-scope pub-sub for cross-component reactivity (no Context, no Redux) — used by `recipeStore`, `MealPlanStore`
- AbortController batched tool registration (W3C spec has no `unregisterTool`)
- IDB schema-versioned upgrade with cascading `if (oldVersion < N)` blocks
- One iframe at a time (single-slot policy enforced by component composition)
